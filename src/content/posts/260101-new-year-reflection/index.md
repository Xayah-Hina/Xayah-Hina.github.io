# Episode 1: Repentance

> "I fell so good, but I'm worn out."
> 
> "We will be all right, don't look so sad."
> 
> "Confess my sin, conceal them all."
> 
> "Night will come soon, and swallow everything."
> 
> --- Sacred Play Secret Place

2025 for me is a year of failures, hurry and regrets.

In the former half, I struggled long time for the papers; for the latter one, I struggled for the PhD entrance issues.
Unfortunately, I failed both.

I think I've tried hard, or maybe not in fact. But now, I feel so tired. Forgive me, my dear self.

I'm such a useless person. I have no talent, no ability. NO "Ling Qi". Maybe I did all wrong in the whole life until
now.

Maybe after several years, I will even lose the young body,young mind and the passion to pursue the dreams. I found
myself so easily get tired.

I think I should directly face my failures, and accept them. No more excuses. That's why I write this repentance.
