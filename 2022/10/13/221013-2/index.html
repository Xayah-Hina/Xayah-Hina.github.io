Writing Practice - 02

2022/10/13 English Writing

Today's topic:

In the future, all cars, buses, and trucks will be driverless. The only people traveling inside these vehicles will be passengers.

Do you think the advantages of driverless vehicles outweigh the disadvantages?

With the rapid development of AI and high-precision sensors, the widespread utilization of autonomous vehicles would inevitably come to reality. In this context, some people are quite looking forward to the convenience that these high-tech vehicles will bring to their lives, but there are also critics expressing their deep concern about its potential security issue and societal impact. From my point of view, I think its benefits will significantly outweigh the drawbacks.

On the one hand, self-driving cars offer unparalleled advantages in terms of daily convenience and travel efficiency. For one thing, commuters could take full advantage of the time spent on the way to study and rest without any driving fatigue, and even the elderly, children, and the disabled are able to take the journey alone to wherever they want. For another, thanks to the AI technology, self-driving cars would automatically choose the best route for travel and are able to control the vehicle more precisely to prevent traffic accidents.

On the other hand, the large-scale use of autonomous vehicles is bound to bring about many social problems. First of all, the security risks caused by the immaturity of these technolegies should be seriously considered, so manufacturing plants must set stringent standards for testing software failure and emergency response capabilities. Besides, jobs like taxi or bus drivers would be redundant and easily get replaced, thus the government should take adequate measures in advance to prevent the resulting mass unemployment issues.

To summarize, although society will go through a painful period during the initial adoption of driverless cars, I believe it is worthwhile to embrace the new technology and make it easier for our lives.

  • P1
    • S1 - background
    • S2 - pros & cons discussion
    • S3 - state opinion: pros > cons
  • P2 : pros
    • S1 - benefits
    • S2 - daily convenience
    • S3 - travel efficiency
  • P3 : cons
    • S1 - problems
    • S2 - technical risk
    • S3 - unemployment issues
  • P4 : summary

大概的一些思想……

一、论述段采用了总分结构……

二、想简单谈谈被动句，也想听听你的想法。【被动句】很多地方都被认为是一种降低readability的句子成分，然后在实际感受上确实感觉有些地方，特别是的确是有明确动作主语的情况下使用被动句会感觉非常别扭。所以怎么说呢，目前的个人感觉，确实应该尽可能避免不合时宜的被动句。（PS. 这个想法我也一直很困惑，事实上很多地方都认为被动句能够提升文章的句法多样性，全文都用主动句也会导致千篇一律，我也觉得有些没必要有主语的地方用被动句更加合适。但这个度一直都感觉都把握不好。而有些网站比如著名的https://readable.com会把所有被动句都圈出来表示降低了readability……）
