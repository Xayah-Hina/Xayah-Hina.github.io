Today's topic:

Many manufactured food and drink products contain high levels of sugar, which causes many health problems. Sugary products should be made more expensive to encourage people to consume less sugar.

Do you agree or disagree?

Manufactured sugary foods and beverages have become ubiquitous and popular in today's society, while many health concerns are provoked simultaneously. Accordingly, some argue that higher prices strategy for sugary products may impel people to turn to a low-sugar diet. However, I don't consider this view comprehensive enough and economically viable. I will discuss my views from the following two aspects.

Firstly, I don't agree higher priced surgery products will eradicate sugar overdependence. For one thing, Coke Zero is comparable in price to standard one while quite many consumers nowadays would spontaneously prefer the former. For another, the price of cigarettes and alcohol has been heavily taxed while it is proved futile in curbing people's addiction. Hence, in my opinion, we need to strengthen publicity to enhance people's awareness of the hazards of sugary products and reduce the cost of substitutes like some Zero Sugar products.

Secondly, I think a crude price increase on sugary products may cause serious unexpected consequences. As one of the most significant and inexpensive sources of energy for human-being, sugar has in fact been an integral part of the nutritional supply. Yet, once prices soar imposed on these necessities, people struggling with poverty would immediately be subjected to brutal starvation and have to suffer a harsher state of being, which would definitely widen the gap between the rich and the poor and even threaten social stability.

In conclusion, I believe that simply raising the price of sugar products will not bring people healthier lifestyle habits. Only a change in people's perception of the hazards of sugar as well as providing quality and inexpensive alternatives could free people from sugar overdependence.

标题逻辑链条

P（【P1】加工食品饮料含高糖->【P2】高糖影响健康） ->Q（【Q1】为糖制品加价->【Q2】鼓励人们不摄入糖）

一共含有三个推理

  • 第一个P1->P2。这个属于题设默认给了的，因此不能辩论。所以着眼点放在后两个。
  • 第二个P2->Q1。大概意思就是推行Q1能够解决P，这里可以作为一个辩论点。比如：非Q1也能解决P2。但这种容易另起炉灶然后离题，所以大部分辩论还是专注一下面这两点。
  • 第三个Q1->Q2。大概意思是推行Q1能够产生Q2的作用（然后解决P），这里可以作为第二个辩论点。比如：1. 反论点 - Q1不能够产生Q2的作用，或者2. 论点片面 - Q1虽然可以产生Q2的作用但是会导致Q3这种更严重的副作用，这个Q3可以是Q1->Q3也可以是Q2->Q3。

大纲

  • P1 目的：复述标题
    • S1 - 陈述背景（即命题，一定是对的不能辩驳的那个）：加糖食品饮料已经在全世界风靡了，引发了极大的健康隐患。
    • S2 - 陈述给的论题（即完整的推论，我们要辩论的主题）：有人主张给糖制品加价，来鼓励人们不摄入糖（然后解决/缓解这个现象）。
    • S3 - 我的观点：我认为加价没有用。
    • S4 - Outline Sentence：我将以以下两个方面来论证。
  • P2 目的：使用反论点攻击Q1->Q2，即加价也没法鼓励人们不摄入糖，问题在更根源的地方。
    • S1 - 提出论点：我不认为高价格能根治糖依赖。
    • S2 - 例子：零度可乐与高糖可乐价格相当，人们会自愿选择零度可乐。
    • S3 - 例子：烟和酒的价格很高宣传也多，但就是很多人抽烟喝酒。
    • S4 - 提出自己的解决方案：加强宣传、降低替代品成本、加强替代品创新
  • P3 目的：使用论点片面攻击Q1->Q2，即加价尽管会让人们吃少糖，但是会引起更大的问题，比如贫困问题等。
    • S1 - 提出论点：加价会导致贫困人口陷入饥饿。
    • S2 - 论据：糖是人重要和且廉价的能量来源非常重要。
    • S3 - 例子：众多对价格敏感的人挣扎在贫困线满足不了每日能量摄取。
    • S4 - 强调观点：加价有危害，不该加价。
  • P4 目的：再次强调自己的观点。

Criterion:

To get an excellent score in the IELTS Task 2 writing section, one of the easiest and most effective tips is structuring your writing in the most solid format. A great argument essay structure may be divided into four paragraphs, which comprise four sentences (excluding the conclusion paragraph, which comprises three sentences).

For us to consider an essay structure a great one, it should be looking like this:

  • Paragraph 1 - Introduction

    • Sentence 1 - Background statement
    • Sentence 2 - Detailed background statement
    • Sentence 3 - Thesis
    • Sentence 4 - Outline sentence

  • Paragraph 2 - First supporting paragraph

    • Sentence 1 - Topic sentence
    • Sentence 2 - Example
    • Sentence 3 - Discussion
    • Sentence 4 - Conclusion

  • Paragraph 3 - Second supporting paragraph

    • Sentence 1 - Topic sentence
    • Sentence 2 - Example
    • Sentence 3 - Discussion
    • Sentence 4 - Conclusion

  • Paragraph 4 - Conclusion

    • Sentence 1 - Summary
    • Sentence 2 - Restatement of thesis
    • Sentence 3 - Prediction or recommendation

Our recommended essay structure above comprises fifteen (15) sentences, which will make your essay approximately 250 to 275 words.
